Files in these folders are converted from NDACC (ftp://ftp.cpc.ncep.noaa.gov/ndacc/station/) to the standard WOUDC extCSV (Extended Comma Separated Values) formats.  


File structures and filenames are the same as NDACC.


May 24th, 2013: Ozonesonde NASA-AMES 2160 format is converted to WOUDC ozonesonde extCSV formats.
Upcoming: Ozonesonde NASA-AMES 2110 format is converted to WOUDC ozonesonde extCSV formats.
